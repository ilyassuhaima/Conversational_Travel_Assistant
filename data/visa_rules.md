# ✈️ Travel Policy Knowledge Base

---

## 🛂 Visa Rules by Passport

### UAE Passport
UAE passport holders can enter Japan visa-free for up to 30 days for tourism purposes. The passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the intended stay. No prior visa application is required. A return or onward ticket may be requested at immigration.

UAE passport holders can travel to France and all Schengen Area countries visa-free for up to 90 days within any 180-day period. Travel insurance covering €30,000 in medical expenses is strongly recommended.

UAE passport holders require a visa to travel to the United Kingdom. A Standard Visitor Visa must be obtained in advance from the UK Visas and Immigration service. The application typically takes 3 weeks and costs approximately £115.

UAE passport holders can enter the United States with an ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization) under the Visa Waiver Program. ESTA applications must be submitted at least 72 hours before travel and cost $21. Approved ESTAs are valid for 2 years or until passport expiry.

UAE passport holders can enter Canada visa-free but must obtain an Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) before flying. The eTA costs CAD $7 and is linked electronically to the passport. It is valid for up to 5 years or until the passport expires.

---

### UK Passport
UK passport holders can enter Japan visa-free for up to 90 days for tourism or business. The passport must be valid for the duration of the stay.

UK passport holders can enter the United States visa-free under the Visa Waiver Program (ESTA). An approved ESTA must be obtained before travel at a cost of $21 and is valid for 2 years.

UK passport holders require a visa to enter the Schengen Area for stays beyond 90 days. For shorter stays (up to 90 days in any 180-day period), UK passport holders can visit Schengen countries visa-free following Brexit.

---

### US Passport
US passport holders can enter Japan visa-free for up to 90 days for tourism or business. Japan may request proof of onward travel.

US passport holders can enter France and all Schengen Area countries visa-free for up to 90 days within any 180-day period.

US passport holders can enter the UAE visa-free and receive a 30-day visa on arrival, extendable once for an additional 30 days. No prior application is required.

---

### Indian Passport
Indian passport holders require a visa to enter Japan. A tourist visa can be applied for at the Japanese Embassy or consulate. Processing typically takes 5–7 business days and requires proof of accommodation and return flights.

Indian passport holders require a Schengen visa to enter France or other Schengen countries. Applications must be submitted at least 15 days before travel. A travel insurance policy of at least €30,000 is mandatory.

Indian passport holders can obtain a UAE visa on arrival for 14 days, or apply for a 30-day or 90-day tourist visa online through the UAE ICP portal. Visa fees vary: 14-day on arrival is free; 30-day single entry costs AED 350.

## 💳 Refund & Cancellation Policies

### Standard Refundable Tickets
Refundable tickets can be canceled up to 48 hours before the scheduled departure time. A processing fee of 10% of the total ticket price will be deducted from the refund. Refunds are typically processed within 7–14 business days and returned to the original payment method.

Cancellations made between 24 and 48 hours before departure on refundable tickets incur a 15% fee. Cancellations made within 24 hours of departure are subject to a 25% fee or may be non-refundable depending on fare class.

### Non-Refundable Tickets
Non-refundable tickets cannot be canceled for a cash refund. However, the ticket value (minus a change fee of approximately $150–$250 depending on the airline) may be retained as a travel credit valid for 12 months from the original purchase date.

In cases of airline-initiated cancellations or schedule changes exceeding 3 hours, passengers are entitled to a full refund regardless of ticket type under most international aviation regulations (EU Regulation 261/2004 for EU carriers, and equivalent rules for US and other carriers).

### No-Show Policy
Passengers who do not check in and do not cancel in advance (no-shows) forfeit the full value of the ticket, including refundable tickets. If the outbound leg of a round-trip ticket is missed, airlines may automatically cancel the return leg.

## 🧳 Baggage Policies

### Economy Class
Most international economy class tickets include one checked bag of up to 23 kg (50 lbs) and one carry-on bag up to 7 kg. Some budget or promotional fares may be baggage-fee fares with no included checked luggage.

Excess baggage fees typically range from $50–$150 per additional bag depending on the airline and route. Overweight baggage (23–32 kg) usually incurs a fee of $50–$100 per bag.

### Business Class
Business class tickets generally include 2 checked bags of up to 32 kg each plus a carry-on and personal item. Some carriers (e.g., Singapore Airlines, Emirates) allow up to 40 kg total for business class passengers.

### Star Alliance Baggage Policy
On Star Alliance-operated codeshare itineraries, the baggage policy of the marketing carrier (the airline that sold the ticket) generally applies end-to-end, even if a partner airline operates a segment. If the ticket is issued on the operating carrier, that carrier's policy applies.

## 🕐 Layover & Transit Rules

### Overnight Layovers
An overnight layover is defined as a connection that requires passengers to remain in the transit area or city for more than 8 hours between 10 PM and 6 AM local time. Some airlines offer free hotel accommodation for overnight layovers on their own-operated connections; this is not guaranteed on codeshare itineraries.

### Transit Visa Requirements
Passengers transiting through the Schengen Area (e.g., Frankfurt, Amsterdam, Paris) require an Airport Transit Visa if their nationality requires one, even if they do not leave the international transit zone. UAE, US, UK, and Canadian passport holders do not require a transit visa for Schengen airports.

Passengers transiting through the United Kingdom may require a Direct Airside Transit Visa (DATV) depending on nationality. Indian passport holders transiting through UK airports require a DATV unless they hold a valid US, Canadian, or Schengen visa.

## ⭐ Airline Alliances Overview

### Star Alliance
Star Alliance is the world's largest airline alliance with 26 member airlines including Lufthansa, Singapore Airlines, Turkish Airlines, ANA, and United Airlines. Members share lounges, allow mileage accrual across carriers, and coordinate schedules for smoother connections.

### oneworld
oneworld includes 13 member airlines such as British Airways, Qatar Airways, American Airlines, Finnair, and Cathay Pacific. The alliance is known for premium service standards and strong transatlantic routes.

### SkyTeam
SkyTeam has 18 member airlines including Air France, KLM, Korean Air, and Delta Airlines. The alliance offers coordinated global coverage especially strong in Europe-Asia connections.

## 📋 General Travel Tips

Travelers are advised to check entry requirements no earlier than 72 hours before departure, as visa-on-arrival and e-visa policies can change with little notice. All passengers should ensure their passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond their return date as a general rule. Travel insurance is strongly recommended for international trips and is mandatory for Schengen visa applications.
